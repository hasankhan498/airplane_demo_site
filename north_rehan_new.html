Good to Know

Frequently asked.

What does First Class include?

A fully enclosed private suite, à la carte dining on your schedule, chauffeur transfer, and a dedicated cabin concierge from takeoff to landing.

Can I change my cabin after booking?

Yes — upgrades are available up to two hours before departure, subject to availability, directly through your itinerary.

Do you offer private charter?

The Aurelia G700 is available for private charter across all long-range routes, with bespoke catering and routing on request.

What is your baggage policy?

First and Business guests enjoy three complimentary checked bags; Premium and Economy include two, each up to 32kg.
